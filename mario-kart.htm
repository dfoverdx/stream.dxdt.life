Hypnotic Mario Kart

While playing Mario Kart, you can spend a few bits to have a message sent to a private Discord channel. dx_over_dt has been hypnotically trained to act on those messages without evening realizing it. It’s a simple as typing one of the commands below and including a cheer with the appropriate amount. Only one command per cheer will be accepted, and there are no refund for cheers that are too small or two large for that command.

Command Bits Message
!banana 20 Run into the next banana you see.
!useitem 5 Use/release your current item.
!closeeyes 25 Close your eyes for 3 seconds.
!rearview 15 Use the rear-view mirror for 5 seconds.
!nodrift 10 Do not drift or break for 15 seconds.
!dodge 25 Dodge item boxes and coins for 10 seconds.
!stop 20 Come to a complete stop.
!norelease 15 Do not release any button you press for 10 seconds.
!ground 30 Place your controller on the ground.
!lookaway 25 Stop looking directly at the screen for 10 seconds.
!fall 30 Do your best to have Lakitu pull you back to the track.
!oranges 30 Oranges
!mute 100 Go mute for 20 seconds.
!360 50 Turn your cart 360° before continuing.

Disclaimer: Hypnosis can be finicky. I will do what I feel compelled to do, and I have tested that compulsion fairly thoroughly, but if I don’t act exactly as instructed, that’s just the way it goes.
